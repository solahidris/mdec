Malaysia Digital Economy Corporation (MDEC), a government agency under the purview of the Ministry of Digital, was established in 1996 to lead Malaysia’s digital economy.

<Cards>
  <Card href="/docs/expats" title="Expats Service">
    The Expats Service Centre is the one-stop hub for processing your company’s Foreign Knowledge Worker (FKW) employment needs.
  </Card>
  <Card href="/docs/mtep" title="Malaysia Tech Entrepreneur Programme (MTEP)">
    MTEP is a specially designed pass to attract tech startup entrepreneur and investor to work or invest in tech industries in Malaysia.
  </Card>
  <Card href="/docs/de-rantau" title="DE Rantau">
    The DE Rantau programme aims at establishing Malaysia as the preferred digital nomad hub in ASEAN.
  </Card>
</Cards>
