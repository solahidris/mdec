## Non-MSC/MD Company Registration

Duration for first time user: 30 WORKING DAYS

<Steps>
<Step>
**Register**  
Create account
</Step>
<Step>
**Verification**  
Received approval/rejection notification after verification process
</Step>
<Step>
**Upload**  
Upload Authorised Person Letter (APL) & Service Agreement
</Step>
<Step>
**Notification**  
Authorised person received notification
</Step>
<Step>
**Complete**  
Registration completed and ready for the next step
</Step>
</Steps>

**Decision**

</Step>

## Approval

Duration: 7 WORKING DAYS

<Steps>
<Step>
**Submission**  
Company submit FKW application & make online payment
</Step>
<Step>
**Verification**  
MDEC verify the details with completed documents
</Step>
<Step>
**Decision**  
MDEC & Immigration either approves or rejects the application
</Step>
<Step>
**Letter Issuance**  
MDEC will issue on Approval/Rejection Letter
</Step>
</Steps>

## Endorsement

Duration: IMMEDIATE

COLLECTION  
@ ESC, KLIA Terminal 1  
(Applicant is NOT in Malaysia)

<Steps>
<Step>
**Submission**  
Company submit application & make online payment  
5 working days prior to arrival in Malaysia
</Step>
<Step>
**Letter Issuance\***  
MDEC process the application & Confirmation Letter will be generated online
</Step>
<Step>
**Collection\*\***  
Applicant enter Malaysia once Reference Visa/eVisa collected at embassy
</Step>
<Step>
**Sticker Issuance**  
Immigration will issue the pass sticker
</Step>
<Step>
**Completion**  
Process completed and applicant is allowed to start work
</Step>
</Steps>

\*The issuance of Confirmation Letter to collect sticker at ESC-KLIA T1, company is required to click the “endorse” button 5 days before the entry/flight date to Malaysia.  
\*\*Applicant to provide physical Approval Letter, Reference Visa/eVisa (if applicable), Confirmation Letter to Immigration in ESC, KLIA T1.

## Endorsement

Duration: 3 WORKING DAYS  

COLLECTION  
@Expats Service Centre  
(Applicant is in Malaysia)

<Steps>
<Step>
**Submission**  
Company submit application & make online payment
</Step>
<Step>
**Sticker Issuance**  
Immigration will issue the pass sticker
</Step>
<Step>
**Collection\***  
Company to collect pass sticker within 5 working days after receiving notification
</Step>
<Step>
**Completion**  
Process completed and applicant is allowed to start work
</Step>
</Steps>

\*For collection of pass sticker, original passport is required (mandatory)
