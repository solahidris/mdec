At the Expats Service Centre, we provide services to aid companies in the process of obtaining valid relevant passes for their Foreign Knowledge Workers (FKW).

In general, companies will go through three (3) pre-application processes prior to applying for the relevant FKW passes.

### Becoming an MD Company

The expatriate services are exclusive to companies that have Malaysia Digital (MD) Company only.

If companies are in the process of applying for Malaysia Digital (MD) Company and waiting for approval, the application’s status can be checked from this online system – [Malaysia Digital platform](https://malaysiadigital.mdec.my/).

Alternatively, companies interested in new MD Company application can find further details on the [Malaysia Digital](https://malaysiadigital.mdec.my/) page.

### Invitation to Subscribe

After the companies are registered as a Malaysia Digital (MD) Company, our Expats Service Centre will send them an invitation email and other relevant documents to register interest in subscribing to our services.

### Acceptance

Upon successful registration, companies will be sent a registration email to initiate the following in the system:

1. To nominate an authorised person(s) on behalf of the company. Companies are discouraged to nominate third party agent.
2. To print and upload a signed copy of the Expatriate Service Agreement.
3. To print and upload a signed copy of an Authorised Person Letter (“APL”) using company’s letterhead for any modifications or additions to authorised personnel. For a change of company name and address, the company MUST provide a new cover letter:
   - Addressed to the Expats Service Centre.
   - Addressed to the Immigration Department of Malaysia in MDEC.
   - The latest e-SSM company profile.

Companies will be notified via email on the next step of the application process.  
Please refer to the [application process](https://mdec.my/expats/application-process-for-md-company) section for the next course of action
