title: About MTEP
description: General information about the Malaysia Tech Entrepreneur Programme.
---

MTEP is a specially designed pass to attract tech startup entrepreneur, experience tech entrepreneur and investor to work or invest in tech industries in Malaysia. Malaysia offers 1-year pass for New Entrepreneurs and a 5-year pass for Established Entrepreneurs or Investors along with dependents.

## Who can apply?

1. **New Entrepreneur**   
   A non-Malaysian individual who may be a self-employed person with an innovative business idea in the digital field/platform and have a strong digital proposition. To qualify, a New Entrepreneur is required to participate by signing up with one of our Malaysia Digital Hubs.   
     
2. **Established Entrepreneur**   
   A non-Malaysian individual who may be a self-employed person that have a strong digital proposition and proven track record (more than 3 years established with the latest 2-year financial records)
