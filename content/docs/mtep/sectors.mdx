## Targeted Sectors & Technology

* AgriTech  
* HealthTech  
* DroneTech  
* FinTech  
* Smart City  
* CleanTech  
* Cyber Security  
* Smart Mobility  
* Artificial Intelligence  
* Blockchain  
* Big Data Analytics  
* Internet of Things

## Non-Qualifying Activities

The following DO NOT fall under MTEP qualifying activities:

1. Trading/Marketplace/Reselling Activity  
2. Manufacturing  
3. Provision of Telco Services  
4. Consultancy  
5. Software Integration  
6. Co-Working Spaces  
7. Services Related Activities (design & support / outsourcing / freelancing services)

\*NOTE:  
If you are bringing in an existing technology or business model into Malaysia, your application should involve reasonable enhancements and additional value-added propositions.
