## Overview

5 year Resident Pass-Established Entrepreneur

* For Founder, co-founders or the Malaysian Venture Partner  
* Minimum funds of RM10mil  
* Must be registered with Securities Commission Malaysia as Venture  
* Capital Management Corporations (VCMC)  
* Eligible to bring dependents

## Requirements

1. Business pitch deck  
   Information that describes your proposed business idea, market opportunity and plans in securing funding or Venture Capital business plan  
2. Financial Tech Record  
   3 months personal bank statement (minimum amount of RM50,000 but not limited to Malaysian banks)  
3. Certificate of good conduct  
   A document issued from the embassy or enforcement agency that states of no criminal record of an applicant in their residing country  
4. Sponsor  
   Declaration Form from a Malaysian sponsor or individual  
5. PROOF OF COMPENSATION  
   A document issued by company with proof of minimum annual compensation of RM 180,000.

## Application Fee

From MYR 5,400
